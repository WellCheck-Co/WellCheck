WellCheck works with two boxes

A floater and an antenna communicating with each other

The floater is placed in any water point (including wells) to send the water measurements to the antenna via radio. It includes an LED lighting in green or red to let locals know if their water is polluted.

What we measure :

- Turbidity (water clarity)
- RedOx (oxygen demand)
- pH level
- Water temperature
